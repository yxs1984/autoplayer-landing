Frequently Asked Questions

Does it work with all video websites?

AutoPlayer works with most major video platforms (YouTube, Vimeo, Dailymotion, educational sites) and many custom video players. The free version supports YouTube and Vimeo, while Premium works with virtually any video site.

Can I schedule videos for specific times?

Yes! Premium users can schedule video sequences to start at specific dates and times. Perfect for planning your learning or entertainment schedule in advance.

What happens when I reach free limits?

You can continue using the free features indefinitely. The 5-video limit applies to sequences - you can create multiple sequences of 5 videos each. Premium removes all limits.

Is there a mobile version?

Currently, AutoPlayer is a browser extension for desktop browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Edge). Mobile support is planned for future updates.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Cancel anytime from your account dashboard. You'll keep Premium features until the end of your billing period, then revert to Free features.
